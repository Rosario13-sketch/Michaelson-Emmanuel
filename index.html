The Vision

Established in 2016, Michaelson Emmanuel Sourcing is a premier garment supply partner based in Dhaka, Bangladesh. We specialize in woven tops, bottoms, and jersey items for a global clientele.

Under the leadership of Michael A. Rosario—who brings over 22 years of industry expertise—we bridge the gap between high-compliance manufacturing and international fashion brands. Our commitment is to quality assurance and seamless production delivery.
